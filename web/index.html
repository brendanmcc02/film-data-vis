MEAN GENRE RATING

>= 5 Films

Nothing incredibly striking or surprising about this graph if I'm honest. It is interesting to see War being ranked as my clear number one. It's impressive to see Drama with a very high rating given that it is my most watched genre (see next graph).

I expected Horror to be one of the lowest ranked. Yes, there are good horror films out there, but for the most part it is a cheap and awful genre. Not surprised by that at all. Musical being my lowest sort of surprises me, but not really in all honesty. I have watched a decent amount of musicals from the 60s and I wasn't too impressed by them.

NUMBER OF FILMS PER GENRE

Similarly, nothing too surprising here. Drama of course takes the number 1 spot; the vast majority of films are classed as a Drama.

A lot more comedy films than I was expecting, but they are all definitely not solely Comedy films, they are probably also Family or Animated films that get classed as Comedies (films can have more than one type of genre associated with it).

TOP 5 DIRECTORS

>= 5 Films

Definitely some surprises here. Before seeing this graph, my 3 favourite directors have always been Nolan, Chazelle, Villeneuve. Nice to see them here.

Linklater has also been a favourite of mine, so wasn't surprised to see him here. The reason his rating is so high is the Before Trilogy is highly rating by me Respectively I gave the 3 films a 10, 9, and a 9. I also gave Boyhood a 9, but School of Rock brings it down with a 7.

Very surprised to see Unkrich here. The reason why he is here is he directed many of the Pixar Films. So it's not necessarily that I love Unkrich as a director, more so that he happened to direct a lot of my favourite Pixar Films (Coco, Toy Story 2 & 3, Finding Nemo, Monsters Inc.).

CHRISTOPHER NOLAN FILMS

My all-time favourite director. Seeing this graph now really emphasises how impressive it is that I love so many of his films. We can kind of see a pattern where his earlier filmography floats between 9's and 7's, then peaks at The Dark Knight and Inception, then levels out at 9's and 8's.

DENIS VILLENEUVE FILMS

(For context, at the time I am writing this I have given all 6 of his films an 8).

This graph is unexpectedly interesting - it's very 'boring', but that itself is fascinating given that I have given all 6 of a Director's films an 8. I'm writing this before Dune Part 2 comes out so this may change.

Initially, I had given Blade Runner 2049, Incendies, and Arrival 9's, but over time the impact a film has on you can either be forgotten or wear off, and that is exactly what happened with those films.

TOP 5 ACTORS

>= 5 Films

Okay here is an interesting one. Morgan Freeman, Cillian Murphy, and Michael Caine are all up there because they are all frequent collaborators with Nolan, who as we have already seen is my favourite director, and has a high mean rating.

Viggo Mortensen has 3 Lord of the Ring films (which I have given all a 9), Captain Fantastic which I gave an 8, and then Green Book which received a 7.

Wallace Shawn voices in 6 Pixar Films, and as we have already seen, those are highly rated. He then features in Marriage Story, which is a film I loved (it got a 9).

RYAN GOSLING FILMS

My favourite actor. I honestly didn't expect his films to be so highly rated across the board. Not much else to add in all honesty.

MY RATING OF IMDB TOP 25

The 25 Highest Rated films on IMDB. You can clearly see some were not my cup of tea (some 6's and 7's here and there). For the most part, it is very highly rated, sporting 3 10's and a handful of 9's and 8's.

IMDB RATING OF MY TOP 10 FILMS

If there's one thing to learn from this graph is that IMDB loves my favourite films. Not one films drops below a mean IMDB Rating of 8.0. Very impressive across the board.

METASCORE OF MY TOP 10 FILMS

Metascore is an aggregate rating of critic's opinions of a film. Like the IMDB Rating graph previously, we learn that critics also love my favourite films. Two of my films even scored the perfect 100.
